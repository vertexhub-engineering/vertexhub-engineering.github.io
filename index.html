HOW IT WORKS

Three tiers.
One standard.

Vertex operates on a structured tier system. Members join for free and pay only for the services they use. Advancement to Senior and VertExpert status is earned through contribution and reputation.

TIER 01 — MEMBER

Create an account

Open to all engineers. Free to join. Access the network directory, community discussions, and pay-per-use access to mentoring, newsletters, and selected content.

TIER 02 — SENIOR MEMBER

Apply & be reviewed

Submit an application reviewed by VertExperts. Senior Members gain priority project access, can publish articles, and are eligible to become VertExperts.

TIER 03 — VERTEXPERT

By invitation only

Invited by founding members or promoted from Senior Member status. VertExperts conduct vetting, offer mentoring, and earn 85% of service fees. Platform retains 15%.

04 — KNOWLEDGE

The vault

Technical articles, case studies, and deep-dives by VertExperts. Some open to Members; premium content is pay-per-access. Not indexed. Not public.

MEMBERSHIP

Three tiers.
Your path into the network.

Joining Vertex is free. Members access the network and pay only for the services they use — mentoring, newsletters, premium content. Advancement to Senior and VertExpert is earned.

TIER 01

Member

Free — create an account

Open to any engineer. No approval required. Access the network directory, community discussions, and pay for the services you actually use.

  • Network directory access
  • Community discussions
  • Free knowledge articles
  • Pay-per-access: mentoring, newsletters, premium articles

TIER 02 — RECOMMENDED

Senior Member

Application-based — reviewed by VertExperts

Submit an application. VertExperts assess your credentials and track record. Senior Members get priority project matching, can publish articles on the platform, and are eligible to become VertExperts.

  • All Member benefits
  • Priority project flow
  • Ability to publish articles on the platform
  • Full access to the premium knowledge vault
  • Private event access
  • Eligible for VertExpert promotion

TIER 03

VertExpert

By invitation or promotion from Senior Member

The inner circle. Invited by the founding team or promoted from Senior Member. VertExperts shape the network, conduct vetting, offer mentoring and newsletters — and keep 85% of service fees.

  • All Senior Member benefits
  • Vetting & screening rights
  • Mentoring revenue (85%)
  • Paid newsletter revenue (85%)
  • Founding circle access
  • Company project mandates

Apply — Senior Member

Applications are reviewed manually by VertExperts. We assess credentials, track record, and technical depth. Not all applicants are accepted.

WHAT WE LOOK FOR

Verifiable expertise

A clear and documented track record. Major projects, institutions, or research that can be independently verified.

Technical depth

Surface-level generalism is not sufficient. We look for engineers who have operated at the boundary of their discipline.

Contribution intent

Vertex is only valuable if members contribute. We prioritise applicants who bring knowledge and are willing to share it.

Professional conduct

The network relies on mutual respect and discretion. References or endorsements from current members significantly strengthen an application.
