HOW IT WORKS

Three tiers.
One standard.

Vertex operates on a structured tier system. Members join for free and pay only for the services they use. Advancement to Senior and VertExpert status is earned through contribution and reputation.

TIER 01 — MEMBER

Create an account

Open to all engineers. Free to join. Access the network directory, community discussions, and pay-per-use access to mentoring, newsletters, and selected content.

TIER 02 — SENIOR MEMBER

Apply & be reviewed

Submit an application reviewed by VertExperts. Senior Members gain priority project access, can publish articles, and are eligible to become VertExperts.

TIER 03 — VERTEXPERT

By invitation only

Invited by founding members or promoted from Senior Member status. VertExperts conduct vetting, offer mentoring, and earn 85% of service fees. Platform retains 15%.

04 — KNOWLEDGE

The vault

Technical articles, case studies, and deep-dives by VertExperts. Some open to Members; premium content is pay-per-access. Not indexed. Not public.

PLATFORM MODEL

Free to join. Pay for what you use.

Members pay for services — mentoring sessions, newsletters, and selected premium articles. Vertex charges a 15% platform fee on all services. VertExperts keep 85%.
